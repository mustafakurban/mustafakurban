Robotics Software Engineer
--------------------------

I am a Robotics Software Engineer at Saha Robotic Delivery Technologies Inc., a company that develops and deploys Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMR) for various industries. My role involves developing control and planning algorithms for AMR using Python, C++, and ROS, as well as simulating and testing them using Gazebo, Airsim, and Carla. Previously, I was a Robotic Software Intern at SK Robotik, where I learned the basics of autonomous vehicle programming and simulation. I also participated in a student team that specialized in autonomous vehicles, and won first place in the Teknofest competition and third place in the Shell autonomous programming competition. I am passionate about advancing autonomous systems and contributing to their applications in various domains. I am always eager to learn new technologies and skills that can help me achieve this goal. I am also a team player who values collaboration and feedback
